HALBERD
BOOKS

Halberd Books is a series of poetry volumes from David M. Briggs. If you like banging your head against the wall, shifting your definitions of love and art, setting your yearning to music, or going a little bonkers, then Halberd Books is for you.

David M. Briggs is a writer, editor, designer, and bookseller from the American Midwest. Over the last two decades, his poetry and prose have regularly appeared online and in print in newspapers, journals, and zines nationwide.

Including Polychrome Ink, Activator Magazine, Springfield Cultural Arts Magazine, The Wisconsin Poets' Calendar, and Steam Ticket: A Third Coast Review.
