Compiled over the course of five years, Halberd Books vol. 1 features some of the most instantly recognizable poetic works of David M. Briggs. From the difficulties of voicing one's feelings to the pernicious beauty of an autumn day, Briggs assembles a mixtape of musical and literary influences and finds a spot to fit.

HALBERD BOOKS VOL. 1 CONTAINS:

  • A Few Too Many Words About Me and You: Notes on Love and Music
  • Speak Easier: Poems Written and Spoken
  • Chain Reactions: Poems from the Catalyst
  • Searching for the Words: Magnet Poems
  • Songs of Captivity and Songs of Escape
  • New and Selected Poems: That May or May Not Have Happened
